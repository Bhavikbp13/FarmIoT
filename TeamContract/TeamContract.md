### Definition of &quot;good enough&quot; for this project

Having a fully functional app by the deadline provided to us. In other words, our app is bug free, super user friendly as well as any additional features we can add in order to make the app as usable and as readable as it could be. Along the way we want each sub system to meet the requirements. Additionally, we want to communicate frequently and our jira board to be updated each day of work so that all team members are always aware of progress. Lastly, to be ready for our sprint meetings a couple days before the deadline.

### Frequency of communication

We would likely communicate daily in order to ensure all team members are moving ahead and have no trouble. In those meetings, we would likely have stand up meetings and update the board. With our different schedules, when working on the project at our own time, we would likely need some type of communication on the weekend so others are aware and can possibly join and work together.

### Response delays

A reasonable response delay would ideally be on the same day. Within 2-4 hours if possible. During the weekend, it would be within the same day but it could also be the next day if the person is available.
